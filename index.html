Start your journey

I

Choose a Sky Sound card that resonates with you and tempts you to explore its soundscapes.

II

For physical editions, scan the card with your smartphone or NFC reader on your PC to access its contents. Discover hidden parameters in for digital cards.

III

Play the music associated with the card to immerse yourself in its sound universe.

IV

Add more cards to your composition. You can include as many cards as your device can handle, further enriching and personalizing your musical experience.

V

Transform the music using the effects and loops provided by the card, creating your own version or musical piece.

This method is designed to be accessible and fun for everyone, from experienced musicians to complete beginners.
